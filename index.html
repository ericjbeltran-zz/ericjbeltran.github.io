About

Borrowing the words of Josh Elman, I help companies and their teams ship the right products to their users. In order to achieve this, I live at the intersection of project management, marketing, technology, business development, user research, user exeperience, customer experience, story telling, client services, service deliver, support, design...you get the picture. Product managers are busy and very cross-functional. The simplify the narrative, Mark Erickson came up with this venn diagram which illustrates and greatly simplifies where it is that product managers live. I could at several spheres to this display but the point is I am a maker and I help companies craft successful solutions. I've worked on digital products for a number of years now and I've lead teams through the journey of solution conception to product launch within web and mobile environments.

Business

I've held positions in both business partnerships and account management. I understand client relationships and what it takes to successfully carry a new relationship through service delivery and across the renewal stage.

Design

I'm a former design and have a an engineers mind but a designers eye. I love participating in design critiques and feel strongly that design is a business function. It is an understatement to say that the marketplace is oversaturated with apps and there is no room for clunky products.

Technology

I've also helped launch a number of products in both web and mobile environments. Understanding how products are built and what it means to make large changes to an existing product is paramount. I put most of my research up-front and craft solutions through and evolutionary prototyping approach.
